The Cost of Mobile Broadband

How do prices for mobile data vary across the world? Just how difficult is it for the average person in another country to afford normal usage?

Comparing the average cost per gigabyte, it's not easy to see any patterns emerging from the map. If we view cost as a percentage of income however, there appears to be a band of darker color starting from the bottom left and extending up towards the right. This covers a good number of lesser developed countries in South America, Africa, and Asia where mobile broadband for the average person is practically out of reach. North America (with the exception of Mexico), Europe, and Australia are much lighter indicating that the average person in these places can easily afford mobile usage.
